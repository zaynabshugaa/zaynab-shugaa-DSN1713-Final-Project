Services and Events

Riverside Art Center offers a dynamic array of services and events designed to inspire creativity and connect our community. From engaging art classes and workshops to captivating exhibits and special events, there’s something for everyone. Join us to explore your artistic side, enjoy local talent, and participate in unique experiences that celebrate the arts.

Classes

Riverside Art Center offers a variety of classes designed to inspire creativity and develop artistic skills for all ages and experience levels. Our courses include painting, drawing, ceramics, and mixed media, led by talented instructors passionate about nurturing creativity. Participants can explore various techniques, experiment with different materials, and express their unique artistic voices in a supportive environment. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced artist, our classes provide opportunities to learn, collaborate, and grow. Join us at Riverside Art Center to discover your passion for art and connect with fellow enthusiasts in a vibrant, welcoming community.

Exhibits

Riverside Art Center hosts a diverse range of exhibits showcasing the talents of local artists, members, and students from nearby schools. These exhibits provide a platform for emerging and established artists to display their work, fostering creativity and community engagement. Visitors can explore a variety of artistic styles and mediums, from paintings and sculptures to photography and mixed media. Our exhibits change throughout the year, ensuring a fresh and exciting experience each time you visit. Join us in celebrating the vibrant art scene at Riverside Art Center and discover the unique stories behind each piece on display.

Events

Riverside Art Center hosts a variety of events throughout the year that celebrate creativity and community engagement. From artist meet-and-greets and gallery openings to workshops and special themed nights, there’s always something exciting happening. Our events aim to connect artists and art lovers, providing opportunities for collaboration and inspiration. We also organize unique experiences like wine tastings, benefit nights, and family-friendly activities, making art accessible to everyone. Join us at Riverside Art Center to immerse yourself in the vibrant art community, support local talent, and enjoy memorable experiences that foster a love for the arts.

COMMUNITY

The Riverside Art Center is a vibrant hub for creativity, fostering a strong sense of community among artists, students, and art enthusiasts. Our welcoming environment encourages collaboration and connection, where individuals from diverse backgrounds come together to share their passion for the arts. Through various programs and events, we cultivate relationships that inspire and support artistic growth. Members have the opportunity to participate in group exhibitions, workshops, and networking events, creating a rich tapestry of creativity and inspiration. The community at RAC not only enhances individual artistic journeys but also strengthens the bonds between artists and their audiences, celebrating the transformative power of art.

This collaborative spirit extends to our outreach programs, which engage local schools and organizations, providing artistic experiences that enrich the lives of community members. By offering classes and workshops tailored for all ages, we empower individuals to explore their creativity while fostering a sense of belonging. Our events, including art fairs and open studio nights, invite the public to experience the artistic process firsthand, allowing for meaningful interactions between artists and the community. At Riverside Art Center, we believe in the power of art to unite, educate, and inspire, making it a cherished space where creativity flourishes and connections thrive.

SHOWCASE YOUR ART

At Riverside Art Center, artists have the opportunity to showcase their work in a supportive and vibrant environment. We invite local creators to display their art through exhibitions, gallery shows, and member showcases. This platform allows artists to connect with the community, gain exposure, and share their unique perspectives. Whether you’re an emerging talent or an established artist, our center provides the resources and support needed to present your work effectively. Join us in celebrating creativity by showcasing your art at Riverside Art Center and becoming part of a thriving artistic community that values and promotes local talent.
